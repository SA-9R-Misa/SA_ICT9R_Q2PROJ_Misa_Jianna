Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • AP Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown and White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough, mix both dry and wet ingredients.
  2. Shape the dough and put in on baking pan that is oiled or greased.
  3. Preheat the oven and bake the cookies at 350°F (175°C) for about 8-12 minutes.
